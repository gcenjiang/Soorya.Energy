Why is solar
right for you

  • With the current price level, the electricity you generate from solar can be 2-3 times cheaper than electricity you get from the grid.
  • Recent government regulation allows for “Net metering”, which means you can lower your electricity bill just by going solar.
  • Solar panel price has dropped by more than 90% for the past 30 years. Adopting solar has become cheap and easy for everyone.

Energy variables

Our calculator will help to calculate the most effective “system size” based on your need. Performance will vary depending on :

  • Weather and climate
  • Roof angle and orientation
  • Shading
  • Quantity and quality of solar panels

We try to reduce the variable by helping you make a better decision.

Financial variables

Your savings will depend on the energy you produce, consume, and sell back to the grid.

Fees can vary depending on :

  • Additional installation expense (for challenging installations)
  • PLN minimum tariff
  • Interest rate from investor
