WHAT IS
SOLAR ENERGY?

  • Solar energy use solar panels made of photovoltaic (PV cells that convert sunlight into direct current (DC) electricity.
  • An inverter converts DC electricity into alternating current (AC) electricity to power up your building.
  • A monitoring system is included in the inverter to send information about the amount of energy your system produces, how much electricity consume, and the amount energy stored into your battery or sold back to the grid.
  • Your solar system is still connected to a local utility grid to provide power after sundown. Connection to the grid also allows your solar system to return to the grid any excess clean energy your system generates during the day. A utility meter measures your power consumption and solar output.
